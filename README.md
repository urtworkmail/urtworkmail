## About Silicate Labs

Silicate Labs is an enterprise software ecosystem headquartered in **Islamabad, Pakistan**. We build intelligence infrastructure, payment rails, logistics platforms, and communication systems — purpose-built for organizations that cannot afford to be second.

Our work spans two tracks: **open-source utilities** that anyone can use and extend freely, and **commercial products** delivering enterprise-grade capability at suitable prices, with free tiers designed to be more than enough to get started. We believe the highest quality software should not be a privilege of geography or scale.

We are actively building out our **Model Context Protocol (MCP) server suite** and expanding our open-source contributions — more OSS is on the way.

## 🟢 Open Source

These repositories are public, free to use, fork, and contribute to.

### 🔗 LinkHub-OSS
**A single HTML file that lets anyone create a beautiful link-in-bio page. No signup. No server. No build step.**

> **Who it's for:** Creators, freelancers, influencers, and anyone who needs a clean personal landing page without depending on third-party platforms or paying monthly fees.

### 🖼️ ImageForge
**Privacy-respecting alternative to remove.bg, TinyPNG, and Canva — runs entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.**

> **Who it's for:** Designers, marketers, content creators, and organizations handling sensitive visual assets that cannot be processed on third-party servers.

![HTML](https://img.shields.io/badge/HTML-E34F26?style=flat&logo=html5&logoColor=white) ![MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green?style=flat)

### 📄 PrivaPDF
**A complete PDF toolkit that runs entirely in your browser. Unlike iLovePDF or Smallpdf, nothing ever leaves your computer.**

> **Who it's for:** Legal professionals, finance teams, HR departments, and anyone handling sensitive documents who cannot accept those documents being processed on external servers.

![HTML](https://img.shields.io/badge/HTML-E34F26?style=flat&logo=html5&logoColor=white) ![MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green?style=flat)

### 📝 FreeResumeMaker

> **Who it's for:** Job seekers, fresh graduates, and professionals who need a polished resume without subscriptions or handing personal data to resume platforms.

We hire in Pakistan, contribute to open source from Pakistan, and build for the market realities that foreign tools consistently get wrong.
